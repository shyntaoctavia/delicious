WAFFOGATO



Ingredients:


  • Waffle Batter
  • Melted butter, for dunking
  • 1/4 c. sugar
  • 2 tbsp. ground cinnamon
  • Vanilla ice cream, for serving
  • Hot coffee, for serving

    • Directions:


    1. Prepare waffle batter and cook in waffle maker. Transfer to a cooling rack while you prepare the sugar dredging station.
    2. Add melted butter to shallow bowl, then dunk waffles in the butter until both sides are evenly coated. In a shallow dish, combine sugar and cinnamon. Dunk waffle in cinnamon sugar.
    3. Top waffle with ice cream scoops, then pour hot coffee over the ice cream. Serve immediately.
